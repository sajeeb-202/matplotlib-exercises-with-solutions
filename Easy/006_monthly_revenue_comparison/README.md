## Business Scenario

A Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) company wants to compare the monthly revenue generated by its **Web Platform** and **Mobile Application** during the first six months of 2026.

The management team needs a visualization that clearly compares the revenue trends of both platforms in a single figure.

As a Data Analyst, create a professional line chart to present the comparison.

---

## Dataset

```python
months = ["Jan", "Feb", "Mar", "Apr", "May", "Jun"]

web_revenue = [18, 22, 25, 28, 31, 35]

mobile_revenue = [15, 18, 21, 24, 28, 33]
```

Revenue is measured in **thousand USD**.
